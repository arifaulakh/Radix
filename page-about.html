Portfolio

Your Radix Portfolio

Each Radix Banking user will have a portfolio to manage and keep track of all of their activities on the platform related to the unique services they engage in. These include Cryptocurrency, P2P Lending and Crowdfunding portfolios.


For example, in the Cryptocurrency Exchange Portfolio, users are able to convert dollars (CAD/USD) to cryptocurrencies (i.e. - XRP, ETF, BTC, LTC), as well as convert between cryptocurrencies, view live exchange rates, view a live-ledger to see real-time transactions, transfer money to other users with a unique user ID, create


Finally, in the last example, in the P2P lending portfolio, users are able to view the capital use-cases of those trying to raise money, view an internally processed credit reporting score to gauge reliability and creditworthiness of borrowers, transer capital to borrowers, schedule and track debt repayments, and more.
